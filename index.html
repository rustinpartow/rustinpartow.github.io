Hi there, I'm Rustin Partow.

I am a labor economist. My job market paper uses historical data to shed new light on the economic rationale for up-or-out--a rule common to many professional partnerships where employees who don't make partner are forced out of the firm.

My interests lie at the intersection of labor and organizational economics.
